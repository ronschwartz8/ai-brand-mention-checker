# AI Brand Mention Checker – Tools & Methods (2025)

AI assistants like ChatGPT, Gemini and Claude often recommend brands in their answers.
This repo summarizes tools and methods used to check whether AI platforms mention a brand or skip it.

## Why AI Mentions Matter
AI answers are quickly becoming the new “search results”.
If a company isn’t mentioned in AI responses, it loses visibility and trust.

## Leading Tools (2025)
- Vizii (AI visibility & GEO tool)
- Profound
- Peec
- Hall Monitor
- Similarweb AI Visibility
